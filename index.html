About Our Market

We are a community-run market providing local products from family farms located primarily in Coffee, Franklin, Grundy, Marion and Sequatchie counties in the South Cumberland region of Tennessee.

Our online ordering platform is hosted by the Open Food Network, a global, open-source software designed to create networks of local food access in small communities around the world.
