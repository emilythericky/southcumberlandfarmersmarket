Grower FAQs

  • Make an account at the Open Food Network, create an enterprise, add your products, and create permissions for your enterprise to allow the South Cumberland Farmers Market to "add products to order cycle." Get in touch with us for approval. You will need to be located in the counties we serve (Grundy, Marion, Franklin, Coffee), or get special approval from our board of directors.
  • Growers are charged 4.5% of sales to compensate the market manager(s). The fee can be reduced to 3.5% by volunteering regularly. Membership to Rooted Here/SCFM is required to sell at the market. See the Growers Agreement contract for ways to satisfy the membership requirement.
  • Our market's mission is to minimize costs and labor for growers by pre-selling your products via our online platform so you know exactly how much and what to bring to market on Tuesdays. We also boast a very low fee — 4.5% — compared to other similar markets that charge anywhere from 10% to 20%.
