Navigating the Shopfront

When you enter the OFN shopfront you will see a list of all available products. Products are organized by the blue categories listed on the right — a product can have only one category, but it can have multiple properties, which are listed in gray below the categories. Click on a blue category to see the products available — they will be listed in alphabetical order.

Tip 1. Be sure to select the filter "Featured" each week to see new and unusual market offerings you might want to try.

Tip 2. Search for specific products or your favorite farmers using the Search box.

Tip 3. Search multiple categories or properties at a time. For example, you could select the categories "Vegetables" and "Prepared Meals" and the filters "Certified Organic" and "Woman Owned".

Tip 4. To have your order delivered, select the appropriate delivery option (for a fee) when checking out.

Tip 5. When you are done shopping, click on your cart in the upper right-hand corner, then click checkout at the bottom right. On the checkout page you will have the payment options of online credit card payment or paying by cash or check at pickup.

Tip 6. To find your current order information, or your order/transaction history, locate the orange person icon and click Account. Your complete order history is under Orders and your Orders can be sorted by shopfront under Transactions. Here you may also edit your credit card info and personal info.
